Our Vision

Affordable. Ethical. Personal.

Consulting through a Social Work lense

Our vision is to help individuals, businesses, and non-profits improve quality of care and expand program availability within the community.

Glaiel Consulting was founded with the ethical principles of the social work field in mind. We utilize a critical, ground level, systemic approach to create agency visions and secure program funding. We strive to build an alliance with our clients to provide high quality work that both reflects and matches the passion our clients have for their programs.
